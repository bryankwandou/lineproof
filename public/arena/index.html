The rulebook

How to read the cards, and what each offence on the sheet actually means.

PLAY ON Clean market

Integrity 85 and up. The book is priced sanely and the fair line sits where you'd expect. Nothing to review.

YELLOW CARD Under watch

Integrity 60–84. Something's off — a slow quote, a margin drifting the wrong way. Logged and kept in view.

RED CARD Market sent off

Integrity below 60. A serious offence: an arbitrage window or a stale line on a live match. Flagged loudly.

STALE_QUOTE Time-wasting

The last 1X2 tick is old while the match is live — the book is quoting a price the game has already moved past.

ARB_WINDOW Own goal

Implied probabilities sum below one. A riskless surface on the consensus line — the book is paying out more than it takes in.

SHARP_MOVE Off the ball

The home probability jumped far beyond this fixture's own history — a sharp move worth a second look.

VIG_COLLAPSE Losing shape

The margin fell hard in a single tick. The book's cushion is thinning fast, so the review flags it.
